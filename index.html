About Me

Hi, I'm Akshara Sharma, a passionate Software Developer and AI enthusiast currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering with a specialization in Data Science at Amity University, Noida. With hands-on experience in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Full-Stack Development, I enjoy building impactful solutions that merge innovation with real-world utility.

My recent experiences include developing AI-powered systems at GenTA in South Korea, Thales, and the Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO), where I worked on everything from offensive content detection to large-scale traffic forecasting and intelligent library recommendation systems. My projects have been recognized at prestigious platforms such as the Smart India Hackathon 2024 Grand Finale and the ICUMT International Conference.

I specialize in end-to-end solution development — from building neural network models to deploying full-stack applications. Whether it's accessible AI platforms for neurodivergent users, real-time river water quality monitoring, or scalable content discovery tools, I strive to create technology that is inclusive, efficient, and future-ready.

Let’s connect and build something transformative together.
